This simple mod changes the max trait points available for Stelaris Evolved, inspired by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=165512482]!!More Trait Picks[/url]
The initial mod is not needed but added for credit

[h1]Changes and Additions[/h1]
[list]
[*] Robots get 8 maximum traits
[*] Machines get 9 maximum traits
[*] Biologicals, Bioservants and Replicants get 10 maximum traits
[/list]

[h1]Mod Load Order[/h1]
[list]
[*] Stellaris Evolved - Alpha
[*] Stellaris Evolved + More Trait Picks
[/list]
